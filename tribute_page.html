Jablinski

The eternal speedo-man

Time line of Jablinski's life:

  • 1969 - Born in Santa Mónica, California
  • 1982 - Fall in a pit when he was 13
  • 1992 - Visited the Golden Palace, looking for government files with Scully and Mulder
  • 1993 - Join the humanitarian cause for waste collection
  • 1995 - Fly over the catastrophic flood to spread its teachings
  • 1996 - Award-winning researcher, makes multiple TV appearances for his advice on the homeless population. In addition, he helps to stop the large-scale foreign invasion.
  • 1997 - Lost his arm as a result of a tragic cigarette accident
  • 2002 - Suffers from a degenerative disease that considerably increases the size of his fangs
  • 2003 - Continues his humanitarian work, teaching underprivileged children about music and history.
  • 2005 - Helps discover new species in danger of extinction, and preserve them in large cities
  • 2006 - Fight against injustice while preaching the word in New Mexico. After a while he decides to face evil itself.
  • 2008 - He carries out a cultural project of humanitarian reintegration in Passaic, New Jersey. Later he enters the jungles of Vietnam, mimicking the fauna and analyzing the behavior of the pandas
  • 2009 - His career begins to decline, taking jobs that put him in a difficult situation.
  • 2014 - Acquire a chain of pornography
  • 2022 - Suffers an injury to his right leg, tripping over his fangs while running at full speed, wearing his traditional speedo
  • ∞ - Lives in our hearts
